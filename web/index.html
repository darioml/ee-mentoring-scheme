Thanks for your interest in the EEE Tutoring Scheme

We welcome all students in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering to join; either as a Tutee or a Tutor. The scheme is currently oriented around first year courses, but please email us if you would want us to expand this to furhter year groups!

Become a Mentee

  • A different perspective on teaching materials
